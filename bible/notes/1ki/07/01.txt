===== Translation Notes: =====

The author is writing about Solomon's palace.

  * **cubit**  - (See: [[:ti:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **Solomon took thirteen years to build his own palace.**  and **He built the Palace**  - Because Solomon was a king, it is implicit that others did the work for him. (See: [[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **Palace of the Forest of Lebanon**  - Although the word palace is used here, it is not the entire palace, but only one of the buildings of the palace complex.
  * **beam**  - a long piece of strong wood usually used as a support for a roof.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
