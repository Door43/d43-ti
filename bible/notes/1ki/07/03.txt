===== Translation Notes: =====

Some details are given about the structure of the Hall of the Forest of Lebanon.

  * **beam**  - a long piece of strong wood usually used as a support for a roof
  * **verses 4-5**  - The author seems to be clarifying the description of the windows (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **the doors and posts were made square**  - "had rectangular frames" (see UDB)
