===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **these will come from far away, some from the north and the west, and others from the land of Sinim**  - people will return from three directions, not from the east.
  * **Sing, heavens and be joyful, earth; break into singing, you mountains!** - everything should shout joyfully. The sky, the earth and the mountains should all sing!
