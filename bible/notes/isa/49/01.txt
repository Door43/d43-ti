===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you coastlands** - "you people who live on islands in the ocean"
  * **give attention** - "pay attention to what I say!" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **He has made my mouth like a sharp sword** - "He caused my messages to be like a sharp sword" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **he hid me in the shadow of his hand** - "He has protected me by his hand" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]).
  * **in his quiver he has hidden me** - "He protects me like someone protects sharp arrows in a quiver." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
