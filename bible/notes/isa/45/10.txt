===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to scold those who argue with him about what he does. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:45:09|45:9]])
  * **What are you fathering...What are you giving birth to?** - The baby is using questions to scold his parents for what they did. This refers to the people that God created arguing with him for what he does. AT: "You should not be my father...You should not be my mother" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
