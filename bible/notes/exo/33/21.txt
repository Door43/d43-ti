===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Here is a place by me, and you will stand on this rock.** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. Yahweh has prepared a viewing place for Moses. (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **my face will not be seen - ** AT: "You will not see my face" (UDB). (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you will see my back, but my face will not be seen.** - "Moses will see Yahweh walking away from him, so he cannot see Yahweh's face."
