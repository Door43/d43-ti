===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cry**  - AT: "wail" or "call for help"
  * **against any of the Israelites not even a dog will bark**  - AT: "The Israelites will be completely safe". (See: [[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **bark**  - This is the noise that a dog makes.
  * **your servants**  - "Your" refers to Pharaoh, the king of Egypt. (See: Pronouns)
  * **come down to me**  - "Me" refers to Moses who is speaking . (See: Pronouns)
  * **I will go out**  - "I" refers to Moses who is speaking. (See: Pronouns)
  * **he went out from**  - "He" refers to Moses. (See: Pronouns)
