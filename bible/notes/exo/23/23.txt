===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **do as they do**  - "do the same things that they do" or "imitate them"
  * **overthrow**  - "defeat them" or "rebel against their power"
  * **smash**  - "break"
  * **your bread and water**  - AT: "what you eat and drink"
  * **I will remove sickness from among you. - **  AT: " I will keep you healthy."
