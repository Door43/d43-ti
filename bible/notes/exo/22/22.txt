===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **widow**  - "woman whose husband has died"
  * **fatherless child**  - "orphan" or "child with no parents"
  * **my anger will burn**  - "I will become angry"
  * **I will kill you with the sword**  - "I will cause you to be killed in a war" (See: Metonymy)
