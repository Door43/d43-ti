===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I myself** - This refers to Yahweh and no one else. AT: "I, by myself"
  * **you will no longer open your mouth to speak because of your shame **- God is saying that they will not be proud about what they did anymore and they will be embarrassed about what they did.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
