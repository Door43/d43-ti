===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **send out a plague**- "start a plague"
  * **the slain**  - "dead people"
  * **sword is upon her from all around**  - "battles and war will surround her"
  * **pricking briars and painful thorns**  - "bush or shrub with stickers that scratch or poke the skin".  This explains that they will no lonoger bother Israel. (See:Metaphor)
