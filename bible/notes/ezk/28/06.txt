===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says**  -"The Lord syas".
  * **you have set your heart as the heart of God**- "you have put yourself in the place of God". (See:Metaphor)
  * **I will bring strangers upon you**- "I will bring enemy armies to attack you".
  * **terrifying men of the nations-**  "powerful armies from other nations".
  * **they will empty out they swords against the beauty of your wisdom -**  "they will attack your wonderful wisdom". They are not actually wise.
  * **and they will profane your splender - **"they will ruin your beauty".They are not actually spledid.
