===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - "This is what God says."
  * **when I gather the house of Israel**  - "I will bring all the people of Israel together"
  * **sanctified among them in the eyes of the nations**  - "The peoples of the nations will see and know that I, God, am holy." (See:Metonymy)
  * **will live securely** - "live in safety"
  * **despise them **- The word "them" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **they will know **- The word "they" may refer to Israel, the nearby nations or both.
