===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they will send you down to the pit** - "they will put you in a very bad unpleasant place".
  * **die the death of the slain** - "die a very horrible death".
  * **Will you truly say, "I am a god" to the face of the one who slays you?** - "You will not have courage when you face your attacker." This is used to rebuke someone. (See:Rhetoical question).
  * **yet** - Here "yet" means "however," "though" or "but." It is not an expression of time.
  * **the one who pierces you** - the one who kills you by cutting into the body.
  * **death of the uncircumcised by the hand of strangers** -  He has no sign of belonging to God, and will suffer death separated from God and his own people.
  * **I have declared it!** - "I, God, say this is true." This emphasizes that it will happen.
