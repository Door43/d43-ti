===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wealthy with wisdom and skill **- This indicates a great amount of wisdom and skill.
  * **treasuries**  - This is a place where a country's wealth or money is stored.
  * **multiplied**  - "grown"
  * **your heart is arrogant**  - "you think better of yourself than you are"
