===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bring down the high tree **- This means to let the tall trees fall down.
  * **exalt the low tree **- This means to make the low trees grow tall.
  * **I wither the watered tree**  " I" refers to God. God can make a good and healthy tree dry up.
  * **I cause the dried tree to bloom **- God can cause a dead tree to flourish or be healthy
  * **declared - **  This means to make known officially
  * **Then all the trees of the field shall know that I am Yahweh :**  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) This means that even the trees will know Yahweh (God)
