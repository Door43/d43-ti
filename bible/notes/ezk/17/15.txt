===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to acquire horses and a multitude of people **- The king of Jerusalem wants to make a military alliance with the Egyptians.
  * **Shall he prosper? - **  The writer is emphasizing the point that the king will fail in his effort. Alternate phrasing could be: "He will not prosper!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will the one doing these things escape?**  - The writer emphasizes the point that the king will be punished for doing this. Alternate phrasing could be: "The one doing these things will not escape!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **If he violates the covenant then will he escape?****
 - **  The writer again emphasizes the point that the king will not escape. Alternate phrasing could be: "If he violates the covenant, he will not escape!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) "Violate" means to break the terms of the covenant.
  * **As I live!**  - God is swearing on his eternal nature. The word "I" refers to Yahweh (God).
  * **surely in that place of the king who made him king, whose oath he despised, and whose covenant he broke, he will die! In the midst of Babylon! **  - Alternate translation: "in Babylon, where the king of Babylon crowned the king of Judah, the king of Judah will die because of his hatred of the promise he made to the king of Babylon and because he broke the covenant he made with him!"
