===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Vedan and Javan from Uzal **- This reference is unclear. It may be best to follow the national language translation. (See: How to translate names)
  * **iron, cinnamon and calamus**  - These are specific goods worth trading.
  * **Dedan was your trader in fine riding clothing**  - Dedan was an area in Arabia, likely known for horses.
  * **chiefs of Kedar**  - These were the leaders of Kedar, the descendants of one of Ishmael's sons in Arabia.
