===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **emeralds, purple, colored cloth, fine fabric, pearls, and rubies** - These are all expensive goods.
  * **wheat from Minnith, millet, honey, oil, and balsam** - These were various goods available in Judah.
  * **abundance of all your propety** - "having a lot of things to sell".
  * **Helbon** - This was a place near Damascus known for its wine.
  * **Zahar** - This was a place known for wool.
