===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **Lydia** - the name of a nation in Asia Minor. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Libya** - a nation in north Africa that was west of Egypt
  * **your men of war** - "men who fought in your wars" or "serving as your warriors"
  * **they showed your splendor** - "they gave you your splendor"
  * **Helech** - the name of a nation on the southeastern coast of Asia Minor
  * **Gammad** - the name of a place
  * **completed your beauty** - "They made you absolutely beautiful"
