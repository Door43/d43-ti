===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.

  * **They made** - The word "They" refers to the people who built the ship which represents Tyre.
  * **oars** - long pieces of wood that people pull through the water to make the ship move
  * **decks** - the parts of the ship that people can walk on
  * **cypress** - a type of tree that always stays green, can grow very tall, can be very old, and has thick needles as leaves
  * **they overlaid them with ivory** - Possible meanings are 1) "they covered the wood of your decks with ivory" or 2) "they put ivory in the wood of your decks."
  * **ivory** - a white, beautiful, and hard material that is made from the teeth of animals, usually from the tusks of elephants
  * **sails** - large pieces of cloth that move a ship when the wind blows on them
  * **colored linen** - "linen with many colors." See how you translated "linen" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:09:01|9:2]].
  * **that were like your banners** - "to be your banner" or "which was your banner"
  * **banner** - a picture or pattern that represents something else
