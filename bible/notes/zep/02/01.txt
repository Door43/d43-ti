===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rally yourselves together and gather,**  - AT: "Gather yourselves together," or "Come and meet together,"
  * **unashamed nation **- AT: "you shameless nation."
  * **before the decree brings forth**  - v2 - See (Parallelism) AT: "Before your judgment begins,"
  * **before the day passes like the chaff,**  - AT: "before repentance is no longer possible,"
  * **before the fierce anger of Yahweh's wrath comes upon you!**  - "before Yahweh's great anger is released and he punishes you,"
  * **Before the day of Yahweh's wrath comes upon you! **- AT: "yes, before you must endure Yahweh's punishment."
  * **Seek Yahweh,**  - "Go to (or "focus on") Yahweh"
  * **all you humble people on earth who obey his decrees!**  - AT: "all you who are humble and follow his commands!"
  * **Seek righteousness! Seek humility,**  - See (Doublet)  AT: "Try to do what is right with humility," See (UDB)
  * **and perhaps you will be protected**  - AT: "and perhaps he will protect (or"keep") you from the penalty" or See (UDB)
  * **in the day of Yahweh's wrath! **- AT: "on that day when Yahweh's punishment comes (or "arrives").
