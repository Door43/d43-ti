===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk about like blind men **- See (Simile)  The thought is that these people will be confused and not know what to do about the distress they are experiencing. AT: "be confused"
  * **Their blood will be poured out like dust, and their inner parts like dung! **- See (Simile) and (Doublet).  AT: "You will die and your dead bodies will lie rotting on the ground!"
  * **Neither their silver nor their gold will be able to save them**  - See (Idiom). AT: "Your treasures will not help save you"
  * **day of Yahweh's fury!**  - "day of Yahweh's great anger!"
  * **The fire of Yahweh's wrath will consume the whole land,**  - See (Metonym).  AT: "The intensity (or "strength") of Yahweh's great anger will destroy (or "devour") the land totally (or 'completely'),"
  * **for the annihilation that he will bring against all the inhabitants of the land will be terrifying!**  - AT: "for the destruction that Yahweh allows in this land will cause all the people to have great fear."
