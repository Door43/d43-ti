===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **precious** - "very valuable" or "costly"
    * **nothing you desire can compare to her** - "wisdom is better than anything else you desire" (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **She has the length of days in her right hand, in her left hand are riches and honor.** - Here wisdom is personified as a woman with "length of days" in her right hand and "riches and honor" in her right hand. The expression "length of days" means "long life." (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
