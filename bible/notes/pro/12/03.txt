===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A person cannot be established by wickedness **- AT: "No one can become safe and secure by doing what is wicked" (See [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cannot be uprooted **- "are as stable as a tree with deep roots" (UDB) (See:[[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]] and [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **A worthy wife is her husband's crown**  - "A good wife is a sign of honor like a crown for her husband." (See: [[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **she who brings shame is like a disease that rots his bones **- "a wife's shameful acts destroy her husband's influence and happiness like a disease destroys his bones" (See: [[:ti:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
