===== Translation Notes: =====
"Wisdom" is continuing her speech.

"Wisdom" uses parallelism to emphasize what she is saying. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:parallelism]])

    * **Whoever corrects a mocker invites abuse
and whoever reproves a wicked person will be hurt.
Do not reprove a mocker or he will hate you** Correcting a evil person will cause them to hurt you (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:parallelism]])
    * **reprove a wise man and he will love you.
Give instruction to a wise man and he will become wiser still;
teach a righteous man and he will increase in learning.** A wise man likes you to correct them so that they can learn more. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:parallelism]])
    * **reprove**  - gently correcting someone
