===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus uses a parable to teach about repentance and forgiveness. 

  * **Then that servant's master called him** - "Then the king called the first servant"
  * **Should you not have** - "You should have" (See: [[:ti:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
