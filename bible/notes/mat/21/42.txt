===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus quotes the prophets to explain the parable.

  * **Jesus said to them**  - "Jesus said to the chief priests and elders"
  * **Did you never read in the scriptures **- a rhetorical question referring to Psalm 118:22-23 (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **The stone**  - The stone that was rejected by the builders became the most important one. (See: Metaphor)
  * **This was from the Lord**  - "This" refers to the great change God had caused.
