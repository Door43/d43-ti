===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to travel to Jerusalem with his disciples.

  * **village**  - This is a small population center.
  * **donkey **- This is a four-footed animal between about 36 and 50 inches (90 to 130 centimeters) tall; a miniature horse, and mostly used as a carrier of loads.
  * **a colt with her **- "a young donkey with its mother"
  * **Untie them**  - The pronoun "them" refers to the donkey and its colt.
  * **The Lord **- Jesus is referring to himself. (See: First, Second and Third Person)
  * **needs them**  - The pronoun "them" refers to the donkey and the colt.
