====== Ahaziah ======

===== Facts: =====

Ahaziah was the name of two kings; one over Israel, the other over Judah.

  * Ahaziah, King of Isreal, reigned for only two years (850-49 BC), following his father, Ahab. He died from injuries suffered in a fall at his palace and was succeded by his brother Jeroboam.
  * Ahaziah, King of Judah, reigned in 842 BC, following his father, Jehoram.  He was murdered by Jebu and buried in Samaria.
