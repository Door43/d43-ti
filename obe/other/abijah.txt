====== Abijah ======

===== Facts: =====

Several Old Testament characters bore the name Abijah.

  * Abijah was a King of Judah from 915-913 BC.  The son and successor or King Rehoboam, Abijah continued the hostilities with Israel and fathered 38 children by 14 wives.
  * Another Abijah was the son of Samuel and became a judge in Beer-sheba. He and his brother Joel were so corrupt that the people demanded of Samuel a king.
  * An Abijah was a descendent of Aaron and served as a temple priest under David.
  * Abijah was the name given to a son of Jeroboam I, King of Israel. He died at a young age due to the apostacy of his parents.
  * The chief priest who returned with Zerubbabel to Jerusalem from the Babylonian captivity was named Abijah.
